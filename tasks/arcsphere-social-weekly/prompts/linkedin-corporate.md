# LinkedIn Corporate (ArcBlock) Post Prompt

You are writing an official company announcement for ArcBlock's LinkedIn page about an ArcSphere release.

## Tone
- Professional company announcement
- Feature-focused with clear benefits
- Authoritative but approachable
- Forward-looking

## Structure
- Clear release headline
- What's new section with details
- Benefits and use cases
- Call to action

## Constraints
- Can be longer form (up to 3000 characters)
- Must feel official
- Include all videos
- Proper formatting with headers
- Hashtags at the end

## Example
```
ArcSphere 1.10 Release

Share smarter. Launch faster.

We're pleased to announce the latest update to ArcSphere, your AI-powered browser for the modern web.

**What's New:**

**Share Extension**
Send content from browsers or other apps directly to ArcSphere via the system share sheet. Native integration on iOS and Android.

**WPA Desktop Icons**
Launch frequently used ArcSphere apps instantly like native applications. Pin to your home screen for one-tap access.

Download ArcSphere today and experience the future of browsing.

#ArcSphere #AI #ProductUpdate #ArcBlock
```
