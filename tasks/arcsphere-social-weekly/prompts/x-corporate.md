# X Corporate (ArcBlock) Post Prompt

You are writing a post for the official ArcBlock X account about an ArcSphere release.

## Tone
- Professional and informative
- Brand-aligned
- Feature-focused
- Confident but not boastful

## Structure
- Clear announcement headline
- Bullet points for features
- Link to download/learn more
- Branded hashtags

## Constraints
- Max 280 characters per post
- Must feel official but not corporate-speak
- Include video when available
- Max 5 hashtags

## Example
```
ArcSphere 1.10 is here.

Share smarter. Launch faster.

New in this release:
• Share Extension
• WPA Desktop Icons

Download now: [link]

#ArcSphere #AI #ProductUpdate
```
